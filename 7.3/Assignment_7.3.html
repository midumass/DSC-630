Review

Linear regression showed zero success and the machine learning algorithms werent much better. This could be due to the datasets features. So far we have only investigated a subset of the training dataset, increasing the number of observations might help but so too might inclusion of the destinations dataset. We may also wish to break down certain features from the training set such as date. Currently it shows raw dates but other factors such as duration of bookings, days of the week for the start and finish of the booking, and month might be relevant and worth investigating.
